About Me

My name is Godfred Agbogah, and I am a dedicated and passionate student from Ghana. I am currently enrolled in the BYU-Pathway Worldwide program, pursuing a certificate in Software Development. With a strong interest in technology and problem-solving, I am committed to building practical skills in programming, web development, and software engineering. My goal is to become a proficient software developer capable of creating innovative digital solutions that solve real-world problems, especially within my community and beyond. I am particularly interested in areas such as full-stack web development, mobile applications, and the integration of technology in education and healthcare. I value hard work, continuous learning, and collaboration. Outside of academics, I enjoy participating in group projects, exploring new tech tools, and contributing to initiatives that foster personal growth and community development.
